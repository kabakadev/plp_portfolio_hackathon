About Me

Hey I'm Ian Kabaka, a backend-leaning web developer who genuinely enjoys the messier side of building things. Solving bugs? I love it. Debugging is where I thrive, especially when a project starts taking shape and those stubborn errors begin showing up, that's when I zone in, sometimes even forgetting to eat.

I'm especially drawn to APIs. There's just something satisfying about sending a request through Postman and getting that green status ... like watching a cold, static system spark to life. I believe building software should be creative, not rigid. So I'm all for mixing things up beyond the usual vanilla routes.

I enjoy working with people, especially in collaborative environments like hackathons or open projects with a community vibe. I've been told I have an eye for detail ... probably because I love breaking down complex problems and getting things just right.

Outside of tech, you'll probably find me playing darts, diving into sci-fi worlds, or vibing with my love for freeform dreadlocks... something about them just speaks to the same raw creativity I try to bring into my code.
